JS Yo-yo is a yo-yo company launched on January 1, 2016. It was founded by Joseph Rock with his friend and fellow Binus University student Stephen Paper. The deployment was initially limited by the founders to Binus students, but was expanded to other in the West Jakarta area, and gradually most in the Jakarta, by March 2016, to everyone in Indonesia without any restriction.
The JS Yo-yo story begins at Binus University. Working in their dorm, Joseph Rock and Stephen Paper created yo-yos product with their brilliant idea and creativity. Soon, they caught attention to several investors. With the investment, they expanded their business and finally, JS Yo-yo was officially born.
Today, with more than 3,000 employees in 3 different countries, JS Yo-yo makes hundreds of products used by millions of people across the world, from basic to professional yo-yos.
